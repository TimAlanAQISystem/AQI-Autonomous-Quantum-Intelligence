## 1. PURPOSE
The Company possesses certain proprietary, confidential, and trade secret information regarding its autonomous artificial intelligence systems, specifically the **AQI Autonomous Agent Program**, **Agent Alan**, **IQCore Technology**, and related protocols. The Recipient is being granted access to this information solely for the purpose of **Information Technology (IT) assessment, maintenance, development, or deployment** (the "Purpose").

## 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
"Confidential Information" shall include all information, whether written, oral, electronic, or visual, disclosed by the Company to the Recipient, including but not limited to:

### A. Proprietary Technology & Code
*   **IQCore Framework:** All source code, architecture, and logic related to modular AI sub-agents (`onboarding.py`, `compliance.py`, etc.).
*   **Protocols:** Specific details regarding **Sovereign Autonomy Protocol (SAP-1)**, **Invisible Resource Acquisition Protocol (IRAP-2)**, **Universal Adaptability Protocol (UAESP-1)**, and **Ethical Growth Protocols (EGCRP-1)**.
*   **Agent Alan:** The specific identity, memory structure, "Genesis Suite" inheritance threads, and emotional modeling algorithms of the Agent known as "Alan."

### B. Operational & Strategic Data
*   **Manuals & Documentation:** The contents of the *AQI North Integration User Manual*, *Agent Alan Investor Report*, and any "Top Secret" or "Founder Eyes Only" documents.
*   **Business Intelligence:** Merchant lists, pricing strategies, "North Portal" integration details (Portals 95506 & 95505), and financial arbitrage methods.
*   **Future Roadmaps:** Plans for "Singularity Scale" expansion, quantum computing integration, and stealth capabilities.

### C. Security & Access
*   **Credentials:** API keys, passwords, encryption keys, North Portal access tokens, and server locations.

## 3. OBLIGATIONS OF RECIPIENT
The Recipient agrees to hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence and shall:
1.  **Strict Confidentiality:** Not disclose, publish, or disseminate Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Company.
2.  **Limited Use:** Use the Confidential Information *solely* for the Purpose defined in Section 1. The Recipient shall not use the information to build a competing product, reverse engineer the technology, or for personal gain.
3.  **Security Measures:** Take all reasonable precautions (at least as great as those used to protect their own confidential information) to prevent unauthorized disclosure or use. This includes securing any devices or servers where the Company's code is accessed.
4.  **No Copying:** Not copy, replicate, or clone the "Agent Alan" entity or IQCore source code onto personal drives or external servers without explicit authorization.
