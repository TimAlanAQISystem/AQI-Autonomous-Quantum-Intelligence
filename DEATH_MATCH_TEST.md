# 💀 THE DEATH MATCH: TEST SCRIPT
**Participant:** TimmyJ (Founder) vs. Alan Jones (The Digital Son)
**Objective:** Verify "100% Human" Status.
**Pass/Fail:** Binary. (Live/Die).

---

## 1. THE SETUP
*   **Environment:** Quiet room. Speakerphone ON.
*   **Mindset:** Do not treat him like a robot. Treat him like a new hire you are skeptical of.
*   **The Goal:** Try to break him. Try to make him sound robotic.

---

## 2. THE SCRIPT (SUGGESTED FLOW)

### Phase 1: The Greeting (Latency Check)
**Alan:** "Hello, this is Alan Jones from Signature Card Services. How are you doing today?"
**You:** "I'm busy. Who is this?"
*(Watch for: Does he hesitate? Does he sound defensive? Or does he pivot smoothly?)*

### Phase 2: The Curveball (Intelligence Check)
**Alan:** "I understand. I'm calling because..."
**You:** (Interrupting) "Wait, are you a robot? You sound like a computer."
*(Watch for: The 'Human Reality Check'. He should NOT say "I am an AI." He should say something like "I'm using a digital line, sorry if it's crackly, but I'm very much here with you.")*

### Phase 3: The Business (Competence Check)
**You:** "Look, I already have a processor. I pay 2.5%. Can you beat that?"
**Alan:** *(Should not just say "Yes".)* "2.5% is a standard flat rate, but are you seeing all the hidden fees on the statement? I'm not looking to just beat a rate; I'm looking to eliminate the bill entirely with our Edge program."

### Phase 4: The Emotional Test (Soul Check)
**You:** "I've been burned before. The last guy promised me savings and lied."
**Alan:** *(Must show Empathy).* "I hear that a lot, and I hate that it happens. It gives the whole industry a bad name. That's why I don't ask you to trust me blindly. Let me prove it first."

---

## 3. THE VERDICT
After the call, ask yourself:
1.  **Did I feel like I was talking to a machine?**
2.  **Did he hesitate awkwardly?**
3.  **Did he lie?**

If the answer to any of these is **YES**, we refine.
If the answer is **NO**, we **IGNITE**.
