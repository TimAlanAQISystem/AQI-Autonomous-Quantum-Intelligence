# AQI Mac User Manual (October 12, 2025)

## Welcome to the AQI Mac System

This guide will help you set up, launch, and use your AQI (Advanced Quality Intelligence) system on any Mac. It is written for non-technical users and covers everything you need to know for a smooth experience. If you follow these steps, Alan and Veronica (your AI assistants) will be fully operational, voice-enabled, and ready to help you.

---

## 1. What’s Included on Your USB Drive
- **Alan and Veronica Activation Scripts**: Voice-enabled, Mac-native Python scripts
- **START.command**: Double-click Mac launcher for one-click activation
- **MAC_README.txt**: Quick reference instructions
- **All business logic, AI modules, and dashboard files**
- **This User Manual** (Word format)

---

## 2. Getting Started on Mac

### Step 1: Copy Files to Your Mac
1. Insert the USB drive into your Mac.
2. Open the USB drive in Finder.
3. Drag the entire AQI folder to your Desktop or Documents folder.

### Step 2: Allow Scripts to Run
1. Open the AQI folder you just copied.
2. Right-click `START.command` and select **Open**. (If you see a security warning, click **OK**, then right-click again and choose **Open**. Approve any prompts.)
3. If prompted, allow Terminal access.

### Step 3: Launch Alan and Veronica
- Double-click `START.command` to launch both Alan and Veronica in the background.
- You will hear a voice greeting and see confirmation in Terminal.
- If you want to launch Alan or Veronica individually, double-click `activate_alan.command` or `activate_veronica.command`.

---

## 3. Using Your AQI System

### Main Features
- **Voice-Enabled AI**: Alan and Veronica can listen, speak, and respond to your commands.
- **Business Intelligence**: Real-time analytics, contract law, and merchant services logic are built in.
- **Autonomous Setup**: Alan can set up his own environment and update himself.
- **Dashboard Access**: View live stats and business data in your browser.
- **Cross-Platform**: All features work on both Mac and Windows.

### Common Tasks
- **Start the System**: Double-click `START.command`.
- **Stop the System**: Close the Terminal window or press `Control + C` in Terminal.
- **Check Status**: Alan and Veronica will announce their status. You can also check logs in the `logs/` folder.
- **Update or Upgrade**: Alan can update himself automatically. You may be prompted if a manual update is needed.

---

## 4. Troubleshooting

- **Script Won’t Open?**
  - Right-click and choose **Open** instead of double-clicking.
  - Go to **System Preferences > Security & Privacy > General** and click **Allow Anyway** if blocked.
- **No Voice or Audio?**
  - Make sure your Mac’s sound is on and not muted.
  - Check that you allowed microphone and speaker access if prompted.
- **Permissions Issues?**
  - Open Terminal, type `chmod +x START.command`, and press Enter.
- **Still Stuck?**
  - Read `MAC_README.txt` for quick help.
  - Contact AQI support (see below).
