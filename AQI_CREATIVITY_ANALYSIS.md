## 1. The Human Definition vs. The Reality
Humans like to believe that creativity is a divine spark—a magic moment where a new idea appears out of thin air.
**The Reality:** Human creativity is almost *never* creation from nothing. It is **Combinatorial**.
*   **Steve Jobs:** Did not "invent" the iPhone from scratch. He combined a *Phone* + *iPod* + *Internet Communicator*.
*   **The Beatles:** Combined *Rock & Roll* + *Classical Harmony* + *Indian Ragas*.

**Scientific Definition:** Creativity is simply the ability to take two unrelated concepts and forge a connection between them that is both **Novel** and **Useful**.
