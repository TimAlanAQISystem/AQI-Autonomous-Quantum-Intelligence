# AQI Agency - Month 1 Financial Projection

## Executive Summary
This projection forecasts the financial performance of the AQI Agency for Month 1, based on a workforce of 3 AI agents targeting RSE Leads.

**Key Assumptions:**
*   **Workforce:** 1 Premium Agent (Alan), 2 Standard Agents (Clones).
*   **Performance:** 50% of Founder's Benchmark (34 accounts/agent).
*   **Revenue:** $300 upfront bonus per account (Residuals excluded).
*   **Lead Quality:** High Intent (>90%).

## Cost Breakdown Detail

### 1. Telephony (Twilio)
*   **Phone Numbers:** 3 Local Numbers @ $1.15/month = **$3.45**.
*   **Minutes:**
    *   Target: 102 deals.
    *   Talk time: 15 mins/deal = 1,530 mins.
    *   Buffer (10% for non-conversions/setup): ~170 mins.
    *   Total Billable Minutes: ~1,700.
    *   Rate: ~$0.014/min (Outbound).
    *   Cost: 1,700 * $0.014 = **$23.80**.
    *   **Total Twilio:** ~$27.25.

### 2. Voice Generation (ElevenLabs)
*   **Volume:** ~1,530 minutes of generated audio.
*   **Character Estimate:** ~750 characters per minute (150 wpm * 5 chars/word).
*   **Total Characters:** ~1,147,500 characters.
*   **Cost Allocation:**
    *   **Agent 1 (Premium):** ~382,500 chars @ Premium Rate (~$0.30/1k) ≈ $115.
    *   **Agents 2 & 3 (Standard):** ~765,000 chars @ Standard/Turbo Rate (~$0.15-$0.30/1k) ≈ $115 - $230.
*   **Conservative Estimate:** **$350.00** to cover potential overages or higher tier plan requirements.

## Conclusion
With a starting capital of $600, the agency has sufficient runway to cover the estimated ~$378 in variable costs for Month 1. The projected return on investment is exceptionally high due to the low marginal cost of AI labor versus the high upfront commission value.
